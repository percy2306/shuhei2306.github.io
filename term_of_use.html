Terms of Use

  1. Disclaimers



Please fill in the [Application Form] if you decide to use our database. We are very keen to hear from people using our database.

This database is free to use for research purposes by academic institutes, companies, or an individual. Any redistribution by you of any content of the database is prohibited. If you seek the use in non-research or commercial purposes, please contact us at aistdancedb@~ in advance.

Please use "AIST Dance Video Database" or "AIST Dance DB" to mention this database and no other expression is acceptable. When mentioning the use of this database in an academic paper, please refer the following paper:

Shuhei Tsuchida, Satoru Fukayama, Masahiro Hamasaki and Masataka Goto. AIST Dance Video Database: Multi-genre, Multi-dancer, and Multi-camera Database for Dance Information Processing. In Proceedings of the 20th International Society for Music Information Retrieval Conference (ISMIR 2019), 2019. 
@inproceedings{aist-dance-db, 
                            author = {S. Tsuchida and S. Fukayama and M. Hamasaki and M. Goto},
